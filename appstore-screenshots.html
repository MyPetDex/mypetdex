Why does Buddy scratch so much in summer?
🐾 PetDex AI
Seasonal allergies are common in Golden Retrievers! Buddy may be reacting to pollen or grass. Try wiping his paws after walks and consider an omega-3 supplement. If it persists, a vet allergy test is a great next step.
What food is best for his coat?
🐾 PetDex AI
For Buddy's coat, look for foods rich in salmon oil, biotin, and zinc. A high-quality fish-based kibble or adding a sardine a week works wonders!
