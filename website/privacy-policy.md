# Privacy Policy

**MyPetDex**
Effective Date: June 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

MyPetDex ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the MyPetDex app (app.mypetdex.app) and marketing website (home.mypetdex.app).

By using MyPetDex, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Pet profiles:** Pet name, species, breed, age, weight, health conditions, vaccination records, feeding schedules, and photos
- **Payment information:** Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers.
- **User-generated content:** Reviews, messages, and other content you submit

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data:** Features used, screens viewed, time spent in the App
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
- **Location data:** Zip code (used for local service provider search). We do not collect precise GPS location.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Stripe:** Payment processing and subscription status
- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication and data storage
- **Anthropic Claude API:** AI-generated nutrition content. Pet profile data (species, breed, age, weight, health notes) may be sent to Anthropic's API to generate recipe suggestions. See Section 4 for details.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide pet profile management and health record features
- Generate personalized AI nutrition recommendations
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Connect you with local service providers based on your zip code
- Send service-related communications (receipts, updates, support)
- Improve the App through usage analytics
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. AI Features and Data Sharing

MyPetDex uses Claude AI (provided by Anthropic) to power the PetDex AI Assistant and Pet Recipes features. When you use these features:

- Limited pet profile data (species, breed, age, weight, general health notes) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses
- **We do not send personally identifiable information (your name, email, or contact details) to Anthropic**
- Anthropic's use of data is governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at anthropic.com
- AI-generated content is not stored by Anthropic after the session ends (subject to Anthropic's data retention policies)

---

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

- **Service providers:** Firebase (Google), Stripe, Anthropic, and Vercel — only as necessary to operate the App
- **Service providers listed on MyPetDex:** If you contact a service provider through the App, relevant information may be shared with them
- **Legal requirements:** If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of MyPetDex or others

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account and pet profile data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., payment records).

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data storage via Firebase (Google Cloud)
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Encrypted passwords (never stored in plain text)
- Stripe-managed payment processing (PCI-compliant)

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and personal data
- **Export** your pet profile data
- **Opt out** of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at **support@mypetdex.app**.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

MyPetDex is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact **support@mypetdex.app**.
